# Sales Data Analysis Report

## 1. Introduction
The purpose of this report is to analyze e-commerce sales data to understand product performance and sales trends over time.

---

## 2. Data Preparation
- The dataset was loaded from a CSV file.
- Missing values were checked and removed.
- The Date column was converted into datetime format for analysis.

---

## 3. Data Analysis
The following analyses were performed:
- Total sales calculated for each product.
- Daily total sales calculated to observe trends over time.

---

## 4. Visualization & Findings

### Product-wise Sales
- A bar chart was created to compare total sales across products.
- **Finding:** Phones contribute more to overall revenue than headphones.

### Daily Sales Trend
- A line chart was used to visualize daily sales changes.
- **Finding:** Sales fluctuate across days, indicating varying customer demand.

---

## 5. Key Insights
- Product category has a strong impact on total sales.
- Sales patterns vary by date, suggesting the influence of timing and demand.
- Visualization helps in quickly identifying high-performing products.

---

## 6. Conclusion
The analysis provides valuable insights into sales performance and customer purchasing behavior.  
Such analysis can help businesses improve inventory planning and sales strategies.
